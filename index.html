Tribute to Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

Welcome to this tribute page dedicated to Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, an Indian scientist and the honorable 11th president of India. He was born on October 15, 1931. He played a leading role in developing missile and nuclear weapon projects, which rewarded him with a nickname "The Missile Man."

He was a very simple person who lived an unpretentious lifestyle. He had a keen interest in literature and wrote poems. He never married. He always faced media himself for his failures while letting others address the success. He remained active till the very end. He died while delivering a lecture at the IIM.
